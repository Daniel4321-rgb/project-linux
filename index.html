Meet linux mint:

Linux Mint is a popular Linux operating system because it is easy to use and very stable. Its interface is simple and similar to Windows, so beginners can learn it quickly. It also runs well on older computers and comes with useful programs already installed.

Meet linux arch:

Arch Linux is a powerful, minimalist distro for advanced users who want full control. You build your system from scratch, customize everything, and stay on the cutting edge with its rolling releases. Definitely not for beginners, but perfect for tinkerers who love learning and tweaking.
